Core Features

Everything You Need

A complete platform for intelligent internship matching, student management, and career guidance.

🧠
AI-Powered Matching

Sophisticated algorithm analyzes skills, CGPA, interests, and certifications to compute a precise match score for every internship opportunity.

👤
Student Profile Management

Comprehensive CRUD interface to add, edit, update, and delete student profiles. Store skills, projects, certifications, and more.

📊
Analytics Dashboard

Admin panel with real-time statistics, allocation tracking, department-wise analytics, and comprehensive reports.

🎯
Smart Recommendations

Personalized internship recommendations with match percentages, helping students discover opportunities aligned with their career goals.

📋
Application Tracking

Track student applications end-to-end from submission to allocation. Real-time status updates for students and administrators.

🔒
Secure Data Storage

Student data persisted locally with full CRUD support. Profile editing, resume upload support, and contact management built-in.

Process

How It Works

1
Create Profile

Students add their academic details, skills, certifications, projects, and career interests to the system.

2
AI Analysis

The AI engine analyzes the profile data and computes compatibility scores against all available internship listings.

3
Smart Recommendations

Personalized internship recommendations are generated, ranked by match percentage for easy decision-making.

4
Apply & Track

Students apply directly and track application status. Admins manage allocations and generate reports.
